# Gemini AI Logo Prompt for Dearly

## Main Prompt

Create a professional, elegant logo for "Dearly" - a romantic letter-writing application. The logo should convey warmth, intimacy, and heartfelt expression.

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: Dearly
- **Tagline**: "Express your heart, beautifully"
- **Purpose**: A digital platform for writing and sharing romantic, personalized letters
- **Target Audience**: People in romantic relationships who want to express their feelings beautifully

## Design Requirements

### Style & Aesthetic
- **Overall Feel**: Elegant, romantic, sophisticated, warm, and intimate
- **Design Style**: Modern minimalist with romantic touches, not overly ornate
- **Mood**: Soft, heartfelt, personal, and timeless
- **Avoid**: Cliché romantic symbols (unless done in a unique way), overly decorative elements, childish or cartoonish styles

### Color Palette
- **Primary Colors**: Rose (#f43f5e / rose-500), Pink (#ec4899 / pink-500), Purple (#a855f7 / purple-500)
- **Gradient Preference**: Smooth gradients transitioning from rose → pink → purple
- **Alternative**: Can use monochrome version (rose/pink tones) or single color variations
- **Background**: Should work on both light and dark backgrounds

### Typography
- **Font Style**: Elegant serif font (matching the app's serif typography)
- **Letter Treatment**: Can be custom lettering or elegant typeface
- **Readability**: Must be clear and legible at small sizes (favicon/app icon scale)

### Icon/Graphic Elements (Optional)
Consider incorporating one or more of these elements subtly:
- **Envelope/Letter**: Stylized envelope, letter, or seal (representing the letter-writing aspect)
- **Heart**: Minimalist heart shape, perhaps integrated into a letter or as a subtle accent
- **Pen/Quill**: Elegant writing instrument
- **Wax Seal**: Classic letter seal element
- **Abstract**: Flowing, organic shapes suggesting warmth and connection

### Technical Specifications
- **Format**: Vector-based design (SVG preferred) that scales from favicon (16x16px) to large displays
- **Versions Needed**: 
  - Full logo with wordmark
  - Icon-only version (for app icons, favicons)
  - Horizontal and vertical layouts
- **Simplicity**: Must be recognizable and clear at very small sizes
- **Scalability**: Works well from 16px to 2000px+

### Composition Options
1. **Wordmark with Icon**: "Dearly" text with a small icon/emblem above or beside it
2. **Integrated Design**: Icon integrated into the letter "D" or as part of the word
3. **Monogram**: Elegant "D" monogram that can stand alone
4. **Stacked Layout**: Icon on top, "Dearly" text below

### Inspiration Keywords
- Elegant serif typography
- Romantic letter writing
- Vintage correspondence
- Modern minimalism
- Warm gradients
- Intimate and personal
- Timeless design
- Sophisticated simplicity

## Specific Instructions for Gemini

Generate a logo design for "Dearly" that:
1. Uses a gradient color scheme transitioning from rose (#f43f5e) through pink (#ec4899) to purple (#a855f7)
2. Features elegant serif typography for the word "Dearly"
3. Optionally includes a subtle romantic element (envelope, heart, seal, or pen) that enhances rather than overwhelms the design
4. Works as both a full logo and an icon-only version
5. Maintains clarity and elegance at all sizes
6. Conveys warmth, intimacy, and heartfelt expression
7. Has a modern yet timeless aesthetic

Please provide:
- A detailed description of the logo design
- Suggestions for color variations (full color, monochrome, single color)
- Recommendations for usage contexts (light backgrounds, dark backgrounds, app icons)
- Any additional design elements or variations that would enhance the brand identity
